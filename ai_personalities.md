# AI Personalities & Card Design Guidelines

This document outlines the strategic archetypes for the various factions in **Jovian Cards**. These designs are rooted in the game's lore, emphasizing the conflict between the bureaucratic Inner System, the bio-hacked Outer System, and the creeping cosmic horror of the Void.

---

## 1. Megacorp (Ira Corp)
**Archetype:** *Industrial Aggro / Swarm*
**Lore Basis:** Ruthless efficiency, expendable assets, mass production. Ira Corp views its units as numbers on a spreadsheet.

### AI Personality: "The Liquidator"
*   **Priority:** Face Damage > Board Control.
*   **Behavior:**
    *   **Expendability:** Willing to sacrifice its own units to push damage (e.g., self-destruct mechanics).
    *   **Spam:** Prioritizes playing maximum number of cards per turn rather than holding for value.
    *   **Focus:** Ignores non-Taunt threats unless they are high-value engines. "Maths" the lethal damage.
*   **Card Style:**
    *   **Keyword: `Downsize`:** Sacrifice a friendly unit to gain Energy or Draw.
    *   **Keyword: `Cybernetics`:** Buffs that persist after death (transferred to other units).
    *   **Visuals:** Drones, faceless security guards, sleek but cold corporate aesthetic.

## 2. Jovian Confederation (The Sisters)
**Archetype:** *Midrange / Synergy / "Hero"*
**Lore Basis:** Family bonds, protecting the weak, elite individuals (The Sisters) supported by loyalists.

### AI Personality: "The Matriarch"
*   **Priority:** Unit Preservation > Value Trading.
*   **Behavior:**
    *   **Protection:** Aggressively buffs and heals high-value "Sister" units.
    *   **Phalanx:** Prefers to keep units adjacent (if positioning matters) or alive to trigger "Ally" effects.
    *   **Reactive:** Holds removal (Tactic cards) for the player's biggest threats rather than wasting them on small fry.
*   **Card Style:**
    *   **Keyword: `Unity`:** Trigger an effect if you control another [Jovian] unit.
    *   **Keyword: `Bio-hack`:** Permanent stat buffs that might come with a downside (e.g., "Take 2 damage, gain +4 ATK").
    *   **Visuals:** Unique character art (The Sisters), bright clean lines, medical/scientific aesthetic.

---

## 3. The Voidborn (Cosmic Horror)
**Archetype:** *Control / Combo / Disrupt*
**Lore Basis:** "That-between-gravity," whispers from the deep, madness.

### AI Personality: "The Whispers"
*   **Priority:** Disruption > Survival.
*   **Behavior:**
    *   **Stalling:** Uses freeze/stun/weakness effects to delay the game.
    *   **Hoarding:** Often passes turns with unspent energy to trigger "Bank" effects or prepare for a massive 10-energy drop.
    *   **Cruelty:** Targets the player's hand or deck (discard/mill) rather than the board.
*   **Card Style:**
    *   **Keyword: `Madness`:** Shuffle "Madness" cards (unplayable or harmful) into the player's deck.
    *   **Keyword: `Consume`:** Destroy a unit to heal fully and gain its stats.
    *   **Visuals:** Tentacles, non-Euclidean geometry, dark purples and blacks, glitch effects.

---

## 4. The Republic (Human Navy)
**Archetype:** *Tempo / Glass Cannon*
**Lore Basis:** Overwhelming firepower, rigid structure, fragile morale.

### AI Personality: "The Admiral"
*   **Priority:** Board Control (via Removal).
*   **Behavior:**
    *   **Tactical:** Uses "Strike" cards (spells) to clear the board before deploying units.
    *   **Formation:** Gains bonuses for having a full board or specific unit types (Soldiers + Officers).
    *   **Retreat:** Mechanics that return units to hand to heal them or re-trigger "On Play" effects.
*   **Card Style:**
    *   **Keyword: `Volley`:** Deal damage split among all enemies.
    *   **Keyword: `Reinforce`:** Summon 1/1 Tokens when damaged.
    *   **Visuals:** Uniforms, standard military hardware, grey and navy blue.

---

## 5. Rogue Labs (Bio-Horror)
**Archetype:** *Self-Harm / Mutation*
**Lore Basis:** Unethical experiments, flesh-warping, unstable results.

### AI Personality: "The Surgeon"
*   **Priority:** Evolution.
*   **Behavior:**
    *   **Mutation:** Constantly buffs a single unit into a "Raid Boss" even if it means neglecting others.
    *   **Regeneration:** High healing capabilities.
    *   **Unpredictable:** AI might have a % chance to make a "suboptimal" play that results in a massive random swing.
*   **Card Style:**
    *   **Keyword: `Evolve`:** Transform into a random unit that costs (1) more.
    *   **Keyword: `Decay`:** Unit has huge stats but takes 1 damage every turn.
    *   **Visuals:** Flesh golems, multi-limbed monstrosities, body horror.
