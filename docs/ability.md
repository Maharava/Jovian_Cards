# Keyword Abilities

This document defines the core keywords and mechanics used in **Jovian Cards**.

## Standard Keywords

*   **Guard:** Enemies must attack this unit before attacking non-Guard units or the Commander.
*   **Rush:** This unit can attack immediately on the turn it is played.
*   **Double Attack:** This unit can attack twice per turn.
*   **Snipe:** This unit ignores **Guard** when selecting an attack target.
*   **Slow:** This unit can only attack every other turn.
*   **Stealth:** This unit cannot be targeted by attacks or enemy abilities until it attacks or deals damage.
*   **Lifesteal:** Damage dealt by this unit heals your Commander.

## Faction Mechanics

### Jovian (Synergy & Sustain)
*   **Rage:** When this unit attacks, it gains +1 Attack *before* dealing damage.
*   **Rally:** When triggered (usually Turn End), grants +1 HP and +1 Max HP to a target (permanent buff).
*   **Heal:** Restores current HP up to Max HP.
*   **Decoy:** A 0-Attack unit with **Guard** that serves as a distraction.
*   **Swap:** Switches the position of this unit with another ally, often returning the ally to hand (Bounce) or triggering positional effects.

### Megacorp (Resource & Disruption)
*   **Encourage:** Grants +1 Attack to a random ally at the end of the turn.
*   **Recycle [X]:** When an allied unit dies, draw [X] cards.
*   **Loot [X]:** When this unit kills an enemy unit or hero, draw [X] cards.
*   **Shield:** This unit ignores the **first instance** of damage it takes each turn.
*   **First Strike:** When attacking, this unit deals damage *before* the defender. If the target dies, the attacker takes 0 damage.
*   **Hack [X]:** Reduces an enemy's Attack by [X] at the end of the turn. (Debuff).
*   **Disarm [X]:** Reduces an enemy's Attack by [X] immediately on play.
*   **Regenerate:** This unit restores 2 HP to itself at the end of every turn.
*   **Assassinate:** If this unit deals damage to a target while attacking, the target is instantly destroyed.

## Status Effects
*   **Stun:** The unit cannot attack or use abilities for its next turn.
*   **Weak:** The unit has reduced Attack.
*   **Silenced:** All keywords, abilities, and buffs are removed from the unit.
