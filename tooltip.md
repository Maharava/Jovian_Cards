## Trigger Types

The game supports these trigger types for when abilities activate:
- **onPlay** - When the card is played
- **onDeath** - When the unit dies
- **onTurnStart** - At the start of your turn
- **onTurnEnd** - At the end of your turn
- **onAttack** - When the unit attacks
- **onDamageTaken** - When the unit takes damage
- **onDraw** - When the card is drawn
- **constant** - Always active (passive abilities)
- **passive** - Ongoing effect that applies to other units
